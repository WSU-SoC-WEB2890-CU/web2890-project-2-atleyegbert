With that said, I always try to be understanding and flexible. I know things come up! Please reach out to me if you are unable to attend your appointment and I will try to work with you!

First 2 - 3 Days:
Leave the protective covering on! After the 2 - 3 Days, you are okay to remove it.
First 2 Weeks:
After removing the protective covering, for the first two weeks, only wash your new tattoo with anit-bacterial or fragrance free soap. Make sure to always have clean hands before touching it. Apply balm at least twice a day and more often if skin is dry or itchy. Never itch or pick at your new tattoo.
First 4 Weeks:
For the first 4 weeks, avoid submerging your tattoo in water. Normal showering is fine.
Always:
If your tattoo is exposed to the sun, always cover it with sunscreen.
