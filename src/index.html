About Me

I've cultivated a passion for artistry that has defined my journey as a tattoo artist for over eight years. I specialize in the delicate intricacies of fine line work, the bold contrasts of black and white designs, and the expansive beauty of large floral tattoos. I've honed my craft to bring your visions to life.

I was born and raised in Utah. Beyond the studio walls, I find inspiration in the natural beauty of Utah's outdoors, often venturing into its majestic landscapes with my husband and my two children. While artistry fuels my professional life, family and nature nurture my soul, creating a balanced tapestry that defines who I am both inside and outside of the studio.
